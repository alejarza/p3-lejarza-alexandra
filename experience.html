Educational and Professional Experience

Education

As an educator in higher ed, I tell my students that you will know what your major is when you are excited to read the textbook and happy to go to class. That's how I learned Psychology was for me. I received Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology at the University of Miami with 3 minors in French, English, and Family & Human Services. This track naturally led me to develop an interest in counseling. Making individuals more aware of the influence of mental health on our well-being became a priority. I received a Master of Science degree in Counselor Education at Florida International University.

Working in a college, it made sense to enroll in a doctoral program in Higher Education to help fulfill what I felt was missing in my career. Based on my work as an educator, my qualitative study focused on the lived experiences of female academic advisors. Through semi-structured interviews, the women discussed their career aspirations, interpersonal relationships between coworkers, and their day-to-day duties. In part, the research intended to highlight an important group of people who are sometimes overlooked in higher education research: staff members.

Below is a short summary of the roles I currently hold, as well as some of the skills I have cultivated over the years.

Current Roles

Since 2018, I have been assisting college students with documented disabilities. I review medical records and psychological evaluations and provide recommended accommodations to promote student success. I am often tasked with creating training materials, handouts, and messages that help communicate important information about the department.

I teach a variety of Psychology classes to undergraduate students (my favorite is Human Growth and Development!). Due to the demands of my full-time work schedule and graduate school, I typically teach on Saturday mornings. However, being in the classroom is one of my favorite things and it never feels like a job.

In 2020, I decided to challenge myself so I dove into the world of technology and design. I am currently enrolled in a Master's program through the University of Florida in Mass Communication (with a concentration in Web Design). I plan to finish my degree in the Spring of 2022.
